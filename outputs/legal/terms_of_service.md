## 3. Description of Service

Temptation Token ($TTS) is a **weekly on-chain voting game** deployed on Base blockchain (Ethereum Layer 2). Users:
- Purchase $TTS tokens via Uniswap V2
- Vote on profile submissions by spending $TTS tokens
- Compete to be the top voter on the winning weekly profile
- Earn prizes automatically distributed by smart contract

Prize distribution per round: 35% to the top voter, 35% to the winning profile, 10% to Polaris Project (anti-trafficking charity), and 20% to Blockchain Entertainment LLC. When a profile was submitted through a registered club partner, the split adjusts to 35/35/10/10/10.
