# Temptation Token — Privacy Policy
**Blockchain Entertainment LLC**
**Effective Date: May 1, 2026**
**Last Updated: May 5, 2026**

---

## 1. Overview

Blockchain Entertainment LLC ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") operates app.temptationtoken.io ("Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

**This Platform is designed with privacy in mind.** Core game functions (voting, staking, prize claims) execute entirely on-chain — no account creation, email, or KYC is required.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2a. Automatically Collected
- **Wallet address** — When you connect a wallet, your public blockchain address is received. This is public information by nature of blockchain technology.
- **On-chain transaction data** — All votes, stakes, and prize claims are recorded on the Base blockchain and are publicly visible.
- **Usage data** — Standard web server logs: IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps. Collected anonymously and not linked to wallet addresses.

### 2b. Voluntarily Provided (Profile Submitters)
- **Display name** — The name you choose for your profile
- **Photo** — The image you submit for competition
- **Link URL** — Optional external link (Instagram, OnlyFans, Linktree, etc.)
- **Payout wallet address** — Required to receive prize payments

### 2c. Analytics
We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand aggregate traffic patterns. GA4 data is anonymized and does not identify individual users. You may opt out via browser plugins.

## 3. How We Use Information

| Data | Use |
|------|-----|
| Wallet address | Connect wallet, process votes/stakes, send bonuses, record submissions |
| Profile data | Display in voting rounds, admin moderation |
| On-chain data | Game operation, prize distribution |
| Usage logs | Security monitoring, abuse prevention |

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Profile Photos and Public Display

When you submit a profile:
- Your photo and display name are publicly displayed to all Platform visitors during the active round
- Approved profiles may be displayed in marketing materials (social media posts, blog content) with reasonable notice
- You may request removal of your profile by contacting support@temptationtoken.io — we will remove it from the Platform within 48 hours, but cannot remove blockchain transaction records

## 6. Data Retention

- **Wallet + on-chain data** — Permanently public on blockchain; cannot be deleted
- **Profile submissions** — Retained until you request removal or we remove them at our discretion
- **Server logs** — Retained for 30 days, then deleted
- **Bonus claims** — Retained indefinitely for fraud prevention

---

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:
- HTTPS/TLS encryption for all web traffic
- Supabase row-level security
- Vercel serverless infrastructure
- No storage of private keys (except Marketing wallet private key for bonus sends — stored as Vercel environment variable)

No security measure is 100% effective. Use the Platform at your own risk.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your off-chain profile data (note: on-chain data is permanent)
- **Object** to processing
- **Data portability**

To exercise any right, email support@temptationtoken.io with your wallet address. We will respond within 30 days.

The Platform is **strictly 18+**. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information, contact us immediately.
