DMCA Policy & Takedown Procedure

Blockchain Entertainment LLC
Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Overview

Blockchain Entertainment LLC ("Company") respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users to do the same. In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act of 1998 (17 U.S.C. § 512), we will respond promptly to notices of claimed copyright infringement.

To Submit a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you believe that content on app.temptationtoken.io infringes your copyright, send a written notice to our designated DMCA agent with ALL of the following:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  2. Identification of the infringing material — provide the specific URL(s) on our Platform
  3. Your contact information — name, address, phone number, and email
  4. A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
  5. A statement that the information in the notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner
  6. Your physical or electronic signature
Send takedown notices to: DMCA Agent — Blockchain Entertainment LLC
Email: dmca@temptationtoken.io
(or support@temptationtoken.io marked "DMCA NOTICE")

What Happens Next

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice:

  1. We will review the notice for completeness
  2. If valid, we will remove or disable access to the alleged infringing content within 48 business hours
  3. We will notify the user who submitted the content
  4. The user may submit a counter-notice (see below)

Counter-Notice Procedure

If you believe your content was removed due to mistake or misidentification:

  1. Send a counter-notice to dmca@temptationtoken.io containing:
    • Identification of the removed content and its former URL
    • Your name, address, phone number, and email
    • A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe the removal was in error
    • A statement consenting to jurisdiction of the federal court in your district
    • Your signature
  2. If a valid counter-notice is received, we may restore the content within 10–14 business days unless the original complainant files a court action.

Profile Photo Disputes

For disputes about profile photos specifically:

  • If you appear in a submitted photo without your consent, email support@temptationtoken.io marked "PHOTO REMOVAL REQUEST" — we will remove it within 24 hours
  • DMCA rights apply to original creative works; appearing in a photo does not automatically confer copyright ownership
  • Right of publicity claims (separate from copyright) should be addressed to support@temptationtoken.io

Repeat Infringers

We reserve the right to terminate the accounts of users who are repeat copyright infringers.
