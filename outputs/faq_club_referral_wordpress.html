Club Referral Program

Temptation Token partners with fan clubs, creator communities, and social groups to share a percentage of weekly prize payouts when their members compete and win.

What is the Club Referral Program?

The Club Referral Program lets an organization — a fan club, OnlyFans agency, content creator network, or any community group — register a unique referral code with Temptation Token. When a profile associated with that club wins a weekly round, the club's registered wallet automatically receives 5% of the prize pool, paid on-chain at the same time as all other prize payouts. No manual claims, no invoices — it's built into the smart contract.

How does the prize split work when a club is involved?

The standard prize split is 40% top voter / 40% winning profile / 10% charity / 10% house. When a winning profile is linked to a registered club, the split adjusts to:

Recipient Share
Top Voter 40%
Winning Profile 40%
Polaris Project (charity) 10%
Club / Partner Wallet 5%
House (Operations) 5%

The club's 5% share comes from the house allocation — the profile's 40% and top voter's 40% are not reduced. Profiles without a club code follow the standard 40/40/10/10 split.

How does a profile get linked to a club?

When submitting a profile at app.temptationtoken.io, there is an optional "Club Referral Code" field. Enter your club's assigned code (e.g., TESTNJ) and submit. Once your profile is approved by an admin, the club link is confirmed on-chain automatically — no further action needed from the profile or the club.

How does my club get registered?

Contact us at support@temptationtoken.io or message @TTSGameBot on Telegram with:

  • Your club or organization name
  • A requested referral code (short, memorable — e.g., MIAMI, VAULTCLUB)
  • A Base mainnet wallet address to receive your 5% payouts

Once registered, we'll call setClubWallet on the smart contract with your code and wallet address. All future wins by profiles using your code will pay 5% to your wallet automatically.

Is the club payout automatic?

Yes. Club payouts are handled by the TTSVotingV3b smart contract on Base mainnet as part of the standard settlement process. The moment a round closes and a winner is selected via Chainlink VRF, all prize shares — including the club's 5% — are transferred atomically in the same transaction. No admin action required, no delays, no claiming.

What types of organizations can join?

Any organization that can direct members to submit profiles and promote voting. Common examples:

  • Fan clubs and Discord communities
  • OnlyFans or Fansly creator agencies
  • Social media influencer networks
  • Fitness or lifestyle brand communities
  • Content creator collectives
  • Sports clubs, social clubs, or fraternal organizations

There is no exclusivity requirement — a club can have multiple profiles competing in the same round, and a profile can only be linked to one club code per submission.

Can I verify my club's payouts on-chain?

Yes. Every club payout emits a ClubPayoutSent(roundId, clubCode, clubWallet, amount) event on the voting contract. You can verify any payment at BaseScan → Events. You can also query clubWallets("yourcode") on the contract's Read tab to confirm your wallet is registered.

Can a club change its payout wallet?

Yes. Contact us with your club code and the new wallet address. We'll call setClubWallet again to update the on-chain mapping. The update takes effect immediately — the next round settlement after the update will pay to the new wallet. To deregister a club entirely, the wallet is set to the zero address, which removes the 5% payout and reverts to the standard 40/40/10/10 split for future wins.
