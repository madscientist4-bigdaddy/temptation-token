Fair Winner Selection

Chainlink VRF — Provably Fair

Winner selection uses Chainlink VRF V2.5 (Verifiable Random Function) on Base mainnet. When a round closes, the contract requests a random number from Chainlink's decentralized oracle network. The random number, along with a cryptographic proof, is published on-chain.


Anyone can verify the proof. Even we cannot predict or manipulate the result. The selection algorithm is immutable in the contract. This is the same technology used by major blockchain games and lotteries.
