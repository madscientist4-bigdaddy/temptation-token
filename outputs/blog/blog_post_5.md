# Stake $TTS and Earn Passive Crypto Income Every Week

Buying $TTS is one way to participate in Temptation Token. Staking it is how you build a compounding position that earns regardless of who wins the weekly vote.

Here's everything you need to know about the TTS staking system.

---

## How Staking Works

When you stake $TTS, you lock your tokens in the staking contract. In return:

1. **You earn APR** — a percentage of your staked amount, paid weekly in $TTS
2. **Your votes get multiplied** — your staking tier boosts every vote you cast

If you're a VIP staker and you vote 1,000 $TTS on a profile, it counts as 3,000 $TTS worth of influence. That's a 3x edge over an unstaked voter.

The combination of passive yield + vote multiplier is why serious players stake before they vote.

## APR Calculation Examples

**Bronze — $50 staked at 8% APR:**
- Weekly yield: $50 × 0.08 / 52 = ~$0.08/week
- Annual yield: $50 × 0.08 = $4/year in TTS
- Plus: 1.1x vote multiplier on every vote

**Diamond — $1,000 staked at 32% APR:**
- Weekly yield: $1,000 × 0.32 / 52 = ~$6.15/week
- Annual yield: $1,000 × 0.32 = $320/year in TTS
- Plus: 2x vote multiplier on every vote

**VIP — $5,000 staked at 45% APR:**
- Weekly yield: $5,000 × 0.45 / 52 = ~$43.27/week
- Annual yield: $5,000 × 0.45 = $2,250/year in TTS
- Plus: 3x vote multiplier on every vote

*Note: Yield is paid in $TTS, so actual USD value fluctuates with the token price. APR is calculated on the TTS value of your stake.*

## The Vote Multiplier Advantage

Staking makes your votes worth more. Here's what that means competitively:

**Unstaked voter vs. Diamond staker, both vote 500 TTS on a profile:**
- Unstaked: 500 TTS in tickets
- Diamond (2x): 1,000 TTS in tickets
- Diamond voter has 2x the odds of being the "top voter" and winning 35% of the pool

At VIP (3x), you need to spend 1/3 as much TTS as an unstaked voter to match their influence. Or you spend the same and have 3x the edge.

For consistent players — those who vote every week — the multiplier compounds your winnings significantly over time.

## How to Stake

1. Go to [app.temptationtoken.io](https://app.temptationtoken.io)
2. Connect your Base wallet
3. Click the **Staking** tab
4. Select your tier → enter the amount of TTS to stake
5. Approve the transaction in your wallet → confirm the stake

Your yield accumulates weekly and can be compounded by restaking. Unstaking returns your full principal with no penalty (except a brief lock period — check the current lock terms in the app).
