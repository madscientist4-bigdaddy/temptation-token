# Submit Your Photo to Temptation Token and Compete for Weekly Crypto Prizes

Every week, women on Temptation Token compete in a crypto voting game — and the winner takes home 35% of the prize pool, paid automatically to their crypto wallet.

No agency. No exclusivity contract. No platform fees. You keep everything.

Here's exactly how it works.

---

## What Is Temptation Token?

Temptation Token ($TTS) is a provably fair voting game on Base blockchain. Men buy $TTS tokens and vote on profiles they like. The profile with the most votes at the end of the week wins 35% of the entire prize pool.

Your job: get people to vote for you.

Your advantage: your social following, your personality, your audience — all translate directly into votes and prize money.

## How Much Can You Earn?

The prize pool grows every time someone votes. Here's a rough scale:

Pool size in USD depends on the TTS price at settlement. Check the live price at [DexScreener](https://dexscreener.com/base/0x77Fe188379BEaAd3BCFb26c965c812CEa721ce68).

Prize is sent directly to your wallet within minutes of round close. No request needed. No wait. Automatic.

## How to Submit Your Photo

**Step 1: Get a Base-compatible wallet**

You need a crypto wallet to receive your winnings. Options:
- **MetaMask** (browser extension + mobile) — most popular
- **Coinbase Wallet** — easiest for beginners (no Coinbase account required)
- Any WalletConnect-compatible wallet

Setup takes 5 minutes. You do NOT need to buy any crypto to submit.

**Step 2: Get 5 $TTS for the submission fee**

The submission fee is 5 $TTS (roughly a few cents). New users get a free welcome bonus when they connect their wallet — which is more than enough to cover the submission.

If you need more TTS: buy at [app.temptationtoken.io](https://app.temptationtoken.io) via the Buy/Sell tab (Uniswap on Base).

**Step 3: Submit your photo**

1. Go to [app.temptationtoken.io](https://app.temptationtoken.io)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Click "Submit Profile" tab
4. Enter your display name, a link (Instagram, OnlyFans, Linktree — your choice), and upload your photo
5. Pay 5 TTS submission fee
6. Your submission goes to admin review (usually approved same day)

Once approved, your profile goes live in the current round for voting.

**Step 4: Promote yourself**

Tell your followers to go to [app.temptationtoken.io](https://app.temptationtoken.io) and vote for you. Share your profile link. Ask your community to vote. The more $TTS your profile receives in votes, the better your odds of winning.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is this exclusive?** No. You can be on OnlyFans, Fansly, Instagram, or anywhere else. Submitting to Temptation Token doesn't limit you anywhere else.

**Do I have to do anything after submitting?** Just promote yourself. The platform handles everything else — voting, settlement, payout.

**Can I withdraw my submission?** Yes. Contact support@temptationtoken.io or message @TTSGameBot on Telegram.

**What if I don't win?** Your photo stays in the pool for future rounds (unless you withdraw). You can resubmit each week. Multiple submissions are allowed (up to 3 per week per wallet).

**Is my payout in cash or crypto?** Crypto ($TTS tokens on Base). You can sell $TTS for ETH on Uniswap, then send ETH to any exchange and withdraw as USD.

**Is this safe?** Yes. The contract is audited by Solidproof. The prize distribution is hardcoded — even the developers can't change who gets paid. Prize selection uses Chainlink VRF (provably random).
