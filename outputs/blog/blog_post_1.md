# What Is Temptation Token? The Web3 Hot or Not Game on Base Blockchain Explained

If you've ever wanted to put real money behind your opinions — and actually profit when you're right — Temptation Token was built for you.

**Temptation Token ($TTS)** is a provably fair, vote-to-earn game on Base blockchain where you spend real crypto to vote on photo profiles, and the top voter each week wins 40% of the prize pool. Automatically. On-chain. No middlemen.

It's part Hot or Not, part prediction market, and 100% on-chain.

---

## The Core Concept

Every week, a new voting round opens with a fresh set of approved profiles. You browse the profiles, decide who you think will win, and vote by spending $TTS tokens. The more $TTS you vote on a profile, the more tickets you accumulate. More tickets = bigger share of the weekly prize.

The round runs Monday 12:00 AM EDT through Sunday 11:59 PM EDT. When it closes, Chainlink VRF (a provably random and verifiable system) selects the winning profile based on weighted voting. The top voter on the winning profile — whoever spent the most $TTS on that profile — wins 40% of the entire prize pool. The winning profile's wallet earns the other 40%.

It's skin-in-the-game voting that actually pays.

## How the Game Works — Step by Step

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Go to [app.temptationtoken.io](https://app.temptationtoken.io) and connect any Base-compatible wallet: MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or WalletConnect. No sign-up, no email, no KYC.

### Step 2: Get $TTS

$TTS is the game's native token on Base blockchain. You can buy it directly in the app via Uniswap, or use any DEX that supports Base. New users receive a free welcome bonus of $TTS just for connecting their wallet — enough to start voting immediately.

Contract address: `0x5570eA97d53A53170e973894A9Fa7feb5785d3b9` (Base mainnet)

### Step 3: Browse and Vote

The current round's profiles are displayed in the app. Click any profile, enter the amount of $TTS you want to vote, and confirm the transaction. Minimum 5 $TTS per vote. You can vote on multiple profiles. You can add more votes to a profile at any time during the round, but you cannot remove votes once placed — so choose carefully.

### Step 4: Wait for Settlement

When Sunday night ends, the round closes. Chainlink VRF selects the winner using verifiable randomness. The smart contract automatically distributes the prize pool within minutes:

| Recipient | Share |
|-----------|-------|
| Top Voter (most votes on winning profile) | 40% |
| Winning Profile | 40% |
| Polaris Project (anti-trafficking charity) | 10% |
| Blockchain Entertainment LLC | 10% |

Funds hit your wallet automatically. No claiming, no waiting, no trust required.

## Is It Fair?

Yes — and provably so. Temptation Token uses **Chainlink VRF (Verifiable Random Function)** for winner selection. This means:

1. The winning profile is selected proportionally to votes received — more votes = more tickets = better odds
2. The exact random seed used is published on-chain and verifiable by anyone
3. No one — not even the developers — can manipulate the outcome

The liquidity pool is locked on Team.Finance for 12 months. The smart contract has been audited by Solidproof. The treasury is held in a Gnosis Safe 2/2 multisig.
