# What Is Temptation Token? The Web3 Hot or Not Game on Base Explained

If you've ever wondered what is Temptation Token — you're about to find out everything.

**Temptation Token ($TTS)** is a provably fair, vote-to-earn game on Base blockchain where you spend real crypto to vote on photo profiles, and the top voter each week wins 35% of the prize pool. Automatically. On-chain. No middlemen.

It's part Hot or Not, part prediction market, and 100% on-chain.

Go to [app.temptationtoken.io](https://app.temptationtoken.io) to connect your wallet and start voting in the current round.

---

## What Is Temptation Token — The Core Concept

Every week, a new voting round opens with a fresh set of approved profiles. You browse the profiles, decide who you think will win, and vote by spending $TTS tokens. The more $TTS you vote on a profile, the more tickets you accumulate. More tickets = bigger share of the weekly prize.

The round runs Monday 12:00 AM EDT through Sunday 11:59 PM EDT. When it closes, Chainlink VRF (a provably random and verifiable system) selects the winning profile based on weighted voting. The top voter on the winning profile — whoever spent the most $TTS on that profile — wins 35% of the entire prize pool. The winning profile's wallet earns another 35%.

It's skin-in-the-game voting that actually pays.

## How the Game Works — Step by Step

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Go to [app.temptationtoken.io](https://app.temptationtoken.io) and connect any Base-compatible wallet: MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or WalletConnect. No sign-up, no email, no KYC.

### Step 2: Get $TTS

$TTS is the game's native token on Base blockchain. You can buy it directly in the app via Uniswap, or use any DEX that supports Base. New users receive a **free welcome bonus** of $TTS just for connecting their wallet — enough to start voting immediately.

Contract address: `0x5570eA97d53A53170e973894A9Fa7feb5785d3b9` (Base mainnet)

Minimum vote: 5 $TTS. Each vote costs fractions of a cent in gas on Base.

### Step 3: Browse and Vote

The current round's profiles are displayed in the app. Click any profile, enter the amount of $TTS you want to vote, and confirm the transaction. You can vote on multiple profiles and add more votes to a profile at any time during the round — but you cannot remove votes once placed, so choose carefully.

### Step 4: Wait for Settlement

When Sunday night ends, the round closes. Chainlink VRF selects the winner using verifiable randomness. The smart contract automatically distributes the prize pool within minutes:

| Recipient | Share |
|-----------|-------|
| Top Voter (most votes on winning profile) | 35% |
| Winning Profile | 35% |
| Polaris Project (anti-trafficking charity) | 10% |
| Blockchain Entertainment LLC | 10% |

Funds hit your wallet automatically. No claiming, no waiting, no trust required.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is Temptation Token used for?**
$TTS is used to vote on profiles in weekly rounds. Votes accumulate to give you tickets for winning the 35% top voter prize. You can also stake $TTS to earn APR and voting boosts.

**Is Temptation Token free to play?**
New users receive a free welcome bonus worth approximately $5 in TTS when they connect their wallet. This is enough to vote in your first round with no upfront cost.

**Can I lose money on Temptation Token?**
Yes — $TTS spent on votes is non-refundable. If the profile you voted on doesn't win, you receive no payout. Only the top voter on the winning profile wins the 35% prize. Treat votes as your cost of participation.

**How do I know the game is fair?**
Every winner is selected by Chainlink VRF — a cryptographically verifiable random function. The proof is published on-chain. Even the developers cannot predict or influence the outcome. The smart contract is audited and open source.
