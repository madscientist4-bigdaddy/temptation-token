Temptation Token ($TTS) is the first vote-to-earn cryptocurrency game on Base blockchain (Coinbase's Ethereum Layer 2). Launched in April 2026, it combines social voting with real on-chain stakes.

Rounds run Monday 12:00 AM EDT to Sunday 11:59 PM EDT — exactly one week. Settlement (Chainlink VRF winner selection + automatic prize distribution) happens within minutes of round close. A new round begins immediately after settlement.

Note on EST vs EDT: The schedule is canonical EDT (UTC−4). During winter months (November–March), when clocks shift to EST (UTC−5), rounds close one hour later in UTC terms. This is an unavoidable limitation of Chainlink's UTC-only cron system.

Temptation Token is a skill-influenced voting game, not pure chance. Your vote strategy, which profiles you support, how much you stake, and your staking multiplier all influence your outcome.

Chainlink VRF ensures the winner selection is cryptographically fair — weighted by votes, not purely random. The more $TTS voted on a profile, the higher that profile's odds of winning. Think of it like fantasy sports with crypto prizes — strategy and engagement matter.
