Section 1

Executive Summary

Temptation Token ($TTS) is a weekly on-chain voting game deployed on Base blockchain. Each week, a new round opens with a curated set of approved profiles. Participants purchase $TTS tokens and vote on the profile they believe will win. The top voter on the winning profile earns 40% of the entire prize pool, paid automatically by the smart contract within minutes of round close. The winning profile earns an additional 40%. 10% goes to the Polaris Project (an anti-trafficking nonprofit) and 10% to Blockchain Entertainment LLC.

The game combines the engagement mechanics of social voting with the financial incentives of prediction markets. It is the first application of this model on Base blockchain — and the first to integrate Chainlink VRF for provably fair outcome selection, a Gnosis Safe treasury, and a full staking system with up to 45% APR and 3x vote multipliers. Every piece of infrastructure is verifiable on-chain. This document explains what we've built, why it works, and how to participate.

Section 2

The Problem

Adult entertainment and crypto have coexisted for years but never properly converged. Content creators generate billions in revenue for platforms — and receive a fraction. Fans pay subscription fees that disappear into platform revenue with no upside. Both parties are stuck in a transaction with no alignment.

On the crypto side, "play-to-earn" games promised a solution but consistently failed: either the token economics were unsustainable, the gameplay was shallow, or the "earning" required more capital than most users had. What the market needed was a game where the stakes were real, the outcomes were fair, and the participation curve started low enough to be accessible.

Temptation Token solves both. Creators compete for real weekly prizes. Voters have genuine financial skin in the game. And the mechanism — a weekly voting round with Chainlink VRF settlement — is elegant enough to sustain indefinitely.

Section 3

The Solution

Weekly on-chain voting rounds. Real crypto prizes. NFT trophies. Provably fair via Chainlink VRF.

Every Monday 12:00 AM EDT, a new round begins. Approved profiles compete for votes. When Sunday 11:59 PM EDT arrives, the round closes. Chainlink VRF selects the winner. The smart contract distributes the prize pool automatically. No waiting. No claiming. No trust required.

The result is a game with genuine stakes on all sides: profiles compete for the winning vote count, voters compete to be the top voter on the right profile, and stakers earn passive yield while multiplying their voting edge. Every interaction is on-chain. Every outcome is verifiable. Every payout is automatic.
